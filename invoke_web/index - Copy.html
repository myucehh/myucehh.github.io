INVOKE

by LAW

“Invoke” Is a silent film and photography project by the artist LAW with the objective of exploring how invoked ideas, especially sensuality, develop in human consciousness. By directing actors to interact with objects chosen for their relative lack of obvious inherent sexual appeal, LAW puts these against a sensual backdrop and asks the question, “is sexuality something that comes from us? Is it the observer who breathes life and traits into objects?” “is it possible to be separate from sexuality?”
